PRESENTING Trivia Timeline
(Video-Game Edition)

HOW TO PLAY

  • You will be shown 1 question at a time with 4 possible answers to choose from.
  • When you have selected your answer simply click it.
  • The game will tell you if you were correct or incorrect.
  • One point will be awarded for each correct answer.
  • Repeat the process until all questions have been answered.
  • If you wish to end the game at any point simple click the exit button and return to the main menu.
